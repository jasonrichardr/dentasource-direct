# Handpiece Placement Choices

The Roson Smart Model A3S provides versatile delivery systems to accommodate your specific clinical workflow:

*   **Over-the-Patient Delivery:** Provides ergonomic access directly above the patient.
*   **Swing-Mounted Delivery:** Offers versatile positioning and easy access from either side.
*   **Cart-Mounted Delivery:** Ensures maximum mobility and flexibility within the operatory.
