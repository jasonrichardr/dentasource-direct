# Advanced Features and Components

The Flagship A3 is packed with intelligent hardware and software solutions to streamline your workflow and enhance operational safety.

## 8-Tooth Smile Oral Light (RoLight)
- **Natural Illumination:** Features Philips LED bulbs designed to mimic natural daylight.
- **Customizable:** Adjustable brightness and color temperature settings to reduce eye strain.
- **Precision Control:** Digital control display for exact adjustments.
- **Eye Protection:** Engineered with a comfortable light spot design to protect both the user's and patient's eyes.

## Medical-Grade Color LCD Screen
- **Intuitive Interface:** Easy-to-read human-machine interface.
- **Real-Time Monitoring:** Displays real-time operational status of the dental unit.
- **Smart Diagnostics:** Features a self-check function on startup and error code display for rapid troubleshooting.
- **Enhanced Utilities:** Includes three customizable memory positions for the chair, plus a built-in digital clock and timer.

## Assistant Arm Anti-Collision Function
- **Smart Protection:** Automatically pauses and reverses the chair's movement upon detecting an obstacle.
- **Safety First:** Prevents damage to the equipment and potential injury.
- **Seamless Operation:** Allows for easy obstacle removal without manual intervention, significantly improving operatory safety.

## 5-in-1 Multifunctional Tissue Box
- **Enhanced Organization:** Dual-layer storage solution for cups, tissues, and small items.
- **Durable Construction:** Impact-resistant, wear-resistant, and made from eco-friendly materials.
- **Convenient Design:** Features an innovative slide-mouth design for effortless refilling.

## Dual IR & Gravity Sensing Water System
- **Touch-Free Hygiene:** Automatic water dispensing triggered by cup detection.
- **Efficiency:** Precise volume control minimizes water waste.
- **Seamless Integration:** Beautifully incorporated into the overall design of the dental unit.

## Water Heating System
- **Maximum Comfort:** Instantly provides warm water for the handpieces and the three-way syringe with a single touch.
- **Consistent Temperature:** Reliable temperature control ensures patient comfort, especially during colder seasons.

## Four-Level Adjustable Handpiece Holder
- **Flexible Positioning:** Offers an adjustment range from 30° to 80°.
- **Ergonomic Access:** Customizable positioning allows the dentist to work in their most comfortable, efficient posture.

## One-Key Smart Drainage Position
- **Streamlined Maintenance:** Single-button activation automatically raises the chair to its highest position.
- **Automated Cleaning:** Triggers a comprehensive 5-minute spittoon flushing cycle to simplify end-of-day routines.

# Advanced Micro-electrolysis Disinfection (EOW-TECH)

The A3 features revolutionary EOW-TECH to maintain flawless hygiene.
- **Exceptional Disinfection:** Eliminates 99.9999% of bacteria.
- **Biofilm Prevention:** Actively inhibits biofilm formation, vastly reducing cross-contamination risks.
- **Non-Toxic:** Produces medically-verified active oxygen clusters that are completely safe and irritation-free.
- **Eco-Friendly & Corrosion-Free:** Leaves zero harmful residues (water is the only by-product) and contains no free chlorine ions, protecting metal components from corrosion.
