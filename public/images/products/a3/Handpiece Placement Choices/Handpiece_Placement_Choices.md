# Handpiece Placement Choices

The Flagship Model A3 offers customizable handpiece delivery systems to perfectly align with your specific clinical habits and ergonomic needs. "Your Dental Unit, Your Way."

## Available Configurations:

- **Over-the-Patient (Continental Style):** Ergonomic whip-arm delivery system designed to minimize wrist fatigue and keep essential instruments within easy reach over the patient.
- **Swing-Mounted (Traditional Hanging Style):** A classic hanging hose delivery system mounted on a smooth, flexible arm for reliable, familiar access.
- **Cart-Mounted (Mobile Cart Style):** An independent mobile delivery cart that provides maximum flexibility, allowing unhindered movement around the entire operatory.
