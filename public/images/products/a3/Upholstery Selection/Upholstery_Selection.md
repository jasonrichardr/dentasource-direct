# Upholstery Selections

The Flagship Model A3 prioritizes exceptional patient comfort alongside easy maintenance for your team.

## Available Materials:

- **PU Leather:** Durable and incredibly easy to clean, offering an excellent standard of comfort and longevity.
- **Seamless Microfiber Leather:** An ultra-premium, smooth finish without seams. It guarantees the highest level of hygiene, effortless wipe-downs, and luxurious comfort for the patient.
- **Sewn Microfiber Leather:** High-grade microfiber leather that features elegant stitching, providing a classic, tailored appearance while maintaining superior breathability and a soft touch.
