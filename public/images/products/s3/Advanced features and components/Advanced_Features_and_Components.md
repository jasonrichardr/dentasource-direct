# Advanced Features for Superior Dental Care

## 01 LED Sensor Operation Light
*   **Optimal Illumination:** Delivers a powerful light intensity of ≥35,000 Lux for exceptionally clear visibility.
*   **Flexible Positioning:** Multi-angle rotating capabilities ensure the light can be precisely directed to meet any clinical requirement.
*   **Hygienic Control:** Features touchless on/off operation and stepless intensity control to prevent cross-contamination.
*   **Sterilizable:** Equipped with a detachable handle that is fully autoclavable for maximum infection control.

## 02 Compact Workflow
*   **Maximized Efficiency:** The compact workflow design allows the dentist to quickly and easily reach instruments, the table, and the touchless panel, saving valuable time.
*   **Ample Space:** Both the primary doctor's table and the expanded table provide more than enough space to accommodate all necessary medical supplies during procedures.

## 03 Swiveling & Detachable Spittoon Bowl
*   **Easy Access:** Designed so all surfaces are easily accessible for thorough and rapid cleaning.
*   **Maximum Hygiene:** The spittoon bowl can be effortlessly detached for deep cleaning and high-level disinfection, maintaining a sanitary environment.

## 04 Detachable Suction Filter
*   **Innovative Design:** Features a newly designed suction filter engineered for optimal performance.
*   **Infection Prevention:** Effectively prevents bacteria from accumulating in water inside the unit using a specialized filter net.
*   **Easy Maintenance:** Can be quickly and easily taken out for routine cleaning and sterilization.

## 05 Perfect Hands-Free Operating
*   **Comprehensive Foot Control:** Includes a versatile 4-way foot control that is highly intuitive and remarkably easy to use.
*   **Convenient Functions:** Integrates dedicated buttons for both the cup filler and the rinsing spittoon directly into the foot control system to prevent cross-infection.

## 06 Comfortable Cushion & Flexible Moving Space
*   **Patient Comfort:** Features wide elbow support and a comfortable wrapping design to ensure the patient remains relaxed during long procedures.
*   **Enhanced Teamwork:** Offers a flexible leg room design at the bottom of the chair that significantly enhances the efficient cooperation between the dentist and the nurse.

## 07 Flexible and Comfortable Dentist Stool
*   **Customizable Ergonomics:** Provides robust 6-way adjustment capabilities for a perfectly tailored fit.
*   **Healthy Posture:** The ergonomic design perfectly fits the back and hips, ensuring thigh muscles relax and optimal blood circulation is maintained, preventing fatigue.
*   **Smooth Movement:** Engineered to be both highly flexible and completely soundless.
*   **Easy Setup:** Features an innovative slide-mouth design for incredibly quick and easy installation.

## 08 Multi-Articulated Headrest
*   **Swift Adjustments:** Allows for faster positioning through a simple, one-handed operation utilizing two articulators.
*   **Exceptional Stability:** The multi-joint headrest delivers unparalleled stability, making it exceptionally safe and comfortable, which is particularly beneficial for children and disabled patients.
