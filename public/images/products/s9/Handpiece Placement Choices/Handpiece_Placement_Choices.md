# Handpiece Placement Choices

The Affordable Luxury Model S9 offers diverse and customizable handpiece delivery systems to seamlessly match your specific clinical requirements and ergonomic preferences. "Your Dental Unit, Your Way."

## Available Configurations:

- **Over-the-Patient (Continental Style):** An ergonomic whip-arm delivery system designed to minimize wrist fatigue and keep essential instruments within easy reach directly over the patient.
- **Swing-Mounted (Traditional Hanging Style):** A classic, reliable hanging hose delivery system mounted on a smooth, flexible arm for familiar and effortless access.
- **Cart-Mounted (Mobile Cart Style):** An independent mobile delivery cart that provides maximum spatial flexibility, allowing unhindered movement around the entire operatory.
