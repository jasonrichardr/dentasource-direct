# Roson Classic Model N2+ Dental Chair

## Overview

The Roson Classic Model N2+ dental chair blends durability and modern ergonomics to elevate the standard of patient and practitioner comfort. Designed to support superior dental care, the N2+ offers a sleek, intuitive layout that enhances daily workflows. With its robust construction and advanced functionalities, this model is an ideal solution for dental professionals seeking reliability and refined aesthetics.
