# Roson Elite Model N2 PRO Dental Chair

The Roson Elite Model N2 PRO is a premium dental unit trusted by over 80,000 dental professionals worldwide. It represents a continuous evolution in dental technology, combining ergonomic excellence with intelligent, user-friendly features.

## Key Features

- **Ergonomic Design:** Engineered for maximum comfort, ensuring support for both the dentist and the patient during extended procedures.
- **Intelligent Soft Start/Stop System:** Experience exceptional smoothness. The chair starts and stops without any sudden jolting or jerkiness, providing a calming experience for patients.
- **Medical-Grade Color LCD Display:** Clear, high-definition interface for seamless operation and monitoring.
- **Durable and Easy-to-Clean:** Designed with high-quality materials that withstand rigorous clinical use while being simple to maintain and sanitize.
- **Extensive Color Customization:** Available in a wide variety of colors to perfectly match your clinic's aesthetic.
