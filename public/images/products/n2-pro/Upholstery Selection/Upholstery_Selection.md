# Upholstery Selections

Comfort meets durability. The N2 PRO offers premium upholstery materials designed for patient relaxation and simple clinical maintenance.

## Available Materials:

- **PU Leather:** Durable, easy-to-clean standard upholstery providing excellent value and comfort.
- **Seamless Microfiber Leather:** Ultra-premium, smooth microfiber leather without seams, offering the highest level of hygiene, easy wiping, and luxurious patient comfort.
- **Sewn Microfiber Leather:** High-grade microfiber leather featuring elegant stitching for a classic, sophisticated look with superior breathability and softness.
