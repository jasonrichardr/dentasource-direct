# Handpiece Placement Choices

The Roson Elite Model N2 PRO offers completely customizable instrument delivery systems to perfectly match your preferred clinical workflow. "Your Dental Unit, Your Way."

## Available Configurations:

- **Over-the-Patient (Continental Style):** Ergonomic whip-arm delivery system that minimizes wrist fatigue and keeps instruments easily accessible over the patient's chest.
- **Swing-Mounted (Traditional Hanging Style):** Classic hanging hose delivery system mounted on a flexible swing arm for straightforward, familiar access.
- **Cart-Mounted (Mobile Cart Style):** An independent, mobile delivery cart offering maximum flexibility and unhindered movement around the operatory.
