# Advanced Features and Components

The N2 PRO is equipped with advanced, industry-leading components designed to deliver superior dental care. Each element is crafted to optimize workflow, hygiene, and patient comfort.

## Shield-Inspired Design
Inspired by the shape of a shield, the N2 PRO's brand-new overall design symbolizes uncompromising protection and safety for both the dental practitioner and the patient. 

## Dentist Table
- Features a wide side table (650 × 315 mm) to comfortably accommodate all necessary instruments.
- Equipped with a cleanable plastic protective pad to extend the table's lifespan.
- Integral table handle allows for convenient and highly efficient positioning.
- Includes a swiveling instrument holder with 5 positions, featuring a pre-position for a scaler or electric motor.

## Assistant Table
- Integrated control panel for easy chair movement.
- Wide side table tailored to meet the dynamic needs of the dental assistant.
- Features a 3-way syringe providing warm water.
- Includes a pre-position for a curing light.
- Flexible movement arm ensures convenient positioning during procedures.

## Spittoon & Water Supply
- **Rotatable Ceramic Spittoon:** Provides more space for the assistant. It is 180-degree rotatable and designed for effortless cleaning.
- **Automated Rinsing:** Programmable cup filler and spittoon rinsing system.
- **Comfortable Water Delivery:** Features a constant-temperature warm water system.
- **Hygiene First:** Equipped with an independent, pure disinfectant water supply system.

## Rolight S Dental Light
- Dual-mode control with manual shortcut options.
- Digital adjustments for both illumination intensity and color temperature.
- Infrared, non-inductive (touchless) control for maximum hygiene.
- Powered by high-quality Philips lamp beads.
- Features a conditional breathing lamp indicator.

## Detachable Suction Filter
- Newly designed suction filter prevents bacteria from accumulating inside the water lines.
- Built-in filter net is effortlessly removable for fast and thorough cleaning.
