# Upholstery Selection

The Classic Model N1 is available with a variety of upholstery options, allowing you to prioritize durability, elegance, or maximum infection control based on your clinic's needs:

*   **PU Leather:** Easy to clean and highly durable, this is the reliable standard choice for busy practices.
*   **Sewn Microfiber Leather:** Offers refined aesthetics and supreme tactile comfort with detailed, elegant stitching.
*   **Seamless Microfiber Leather:** Provides a sleek, modern appearance while maximizing clinical hygiene through its seamless, easy-to-sanitize surface.
