# Advanced Features for Superior Dental Care

## 01 8-Tooth Smile Oral Light
*   **Dual Control Options:** Features both a manual shortcut control and an infrared non-inductive (touchless) sensor for exceptional operational flexibility and superior hygiene.
*   **Precision Illumination:** Houses high-quality Philips LED lamp beads providing natural light with a comfortable spot design that protects the eyes.
*   **Adjustable Settings:** Offers digital control over both illumination intensity and color temperature to perfectly suit various dental procedures.

## 02 Dentist Table
*   **Spacious Workspace:** A generously sized side table (650x300mm) meets all the organizational needs of the modern dentist.
*   **Efficient Operation:** Features an integral table handle for convenient handling, plus a swiveling instrument holder optimizing a highly efficient, high-performance workflow.
*   **Hygiene Prioritized:** Includes a cleanable plastic pad to protect the working surface and extend the table's lifespan.
*   **Versatile Capacity:** Equipped with a 5-position handpiece holder, including pre-positioned slots for a scaler or an electric motor.

## 03 Spittoon
*   **Easy Maintenance:** The ceramic spittoon is designed for effortless cleaning and is fully rotatable up to 180 degrees, maximizing functional space for the dental assistant.
*   **Automated Systems:** Includes a highly programmable cup filler and a spittoon rinsing system for seamless operational flow.
*   **Patient Comfort:** Features a constant temperature warm water system, enhancing patient comfort during rinsing.
*   **Pure Water Supply:** Integrated pure water supply system guarantees high standards of clinical hygiene.

## 04 Detachable Suction Filter
*   **Innovative Design:** Utilizes a newly designed suction filter engineered for superior performance.
*   **Bacterial Prevention:** Employs a specialized filter net to effectively prevent bacteria from accumulating in the unit's internal water systems.
*   **Simplified Cleaning:** Designed to be easily and securely taken out for routine, thorough cleaning.

## 05 Multifunction Foot Control
*   **Reliable Operation:** Boasts an exclusive Foshan design with a non-skid bottom plate for secure, low-noise operation.
*   **Enhanced Durability:** A completely new structural design guarantees the foot control has an exceptionally long working life.
*   **Integrated Functions:** Provides convenient water rinsing and supply buttons directly on the pedal, freeing the dentist's hands.

## 06 Dentist Stool (RS03)
*   **Health and Comfort:** The integral backrest design effectively relieves stress on the back and elbows, promoting a healthier posture.
*   **Stability:** Supported by a wide, heavy-duty casting aluminum base, ensuring maximum stability and safety.
*   **Smooth Mobility:** Features high-quality, noiseless nylon wheels for effortless movement around the operatory.

## 07 Assistant Table
*   **Comprehensive Control:** Integrates a complete control panel for precise management of all chair movements.
*   **Enhanced Capability:** A wide side table accommodates all the essential tools and materials needed by the dental assistant.
*   **Essential Tools Included:** Comes standard with a 3-way syringe (warm/cold water) and is pre-positioned to accept a curing light.
*   **Flexible Access:** Highly flexible movement ensures instruments are always conveniently within reach.

## 08 Patient Seat
*   **Superior Support:** The multi-articulated headrest provides exceptional support and is incredibly convenient to adjust for diverse patient needs.
*   **Easy Entry/Exit:** Features a fully rotatable right armrest, ensuring patients can sit down and stand up with total ease.
*   **Simplified Operation:** A single main switch instantly controls the air, water, and electricity, vastly simplifying the start and end of your daily workflow.
